About Us

The Eezi Drive concept is the new way to regain your mobility. It was conceived by a need to the meet needs of clients who are wary of unplanned high maintenance and repair costs or may have an credit record. We provide a solution if you've been declined for traditional finance due to low credit score, being blacklisted, or under debt review.

Our Services

At EeziDrive, we offer Full Maintenance Leasing services designed to give our clients affordable, worry-free access to reliable vehicles. Our clients pay a fixed monthly usage fee that covers:

  • Use of the vehicle
  • All routine maintenance and servicing
  • Repairs (mechanical and electrical)
  • Comprehensive insurance
  • Roadside assistance (where applicable)

This solution is ideal for individuals who want the benefits of vehicle use without the burden of ownership, unpredictable repair costs, or traditional vehicle finance constraints.
